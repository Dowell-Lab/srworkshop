M and M set up

This example will serve ~50 people. It will cost ~$45.

You need

Two bowls
a spoon for mixing
4lbs of mixed M and Ms
1lb of jet puffed miniature marshmallows
3 oz Kraft Jet-Puffed Mallow Bits Vanilla Marshmallows
2-3 oz of skittles
snack size Ziplocs with two pictures (I used frozen and starwars)

 

Mix this in the two bowls

Bowl 1-
2lbs of mixed M and Ms
2-3 oz of skittles
1/2 lb of jet puffed miniature marshmallows
1.5 oz of Kraft Jet-Puffed Mallow Bits Vanilla Marshmallows

Bowl 2- 
2lbs of mixed M and Ms
1 lb of blue M and Ms (or any other color that is already in the mix)
1/2 lb of jet puffed miniature marshmallows
1.5 oz of Kraft Jet-Puffed Mallow Bits Vanilla Marshmallows
